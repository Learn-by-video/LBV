Renewable Energy: A Sustainable Future

A science project about different types of renewable energy, how they work, and their impact on the environment.

Introduction to Renewable Energy

Renewable energy is revolutionary to the energy crisis we have on our hands. It is will never run out unlike Non-renewable energy, plus renewable energy is clean + safe for the environment.

Types of Renewable Energy

  • Solar
  • Wind
  • Hydropower
  • Biomass
  • Geothermal

Impact on the Environment

Most renewable energy is safe to the environment, but some renewable energy affects the environment, like biomass, which creates greenhouse gases that affects the atmosphere, causing global warming.

How It Works

Renewable energy works in different ways; for example, biomass is leftover organic matter that is burned to power generators.

Another form of renewable energy is solar energy. It uses solar panels to power houses and other appliances.

The last form of renewable energy is wind energy. People build turbines that spin when a sufficient amount of wind is blowing past them, generating energy to power equipment.

Renewable Energy Challenges

Some challenges for wind energy is when there isn't enough wind to spin the turbine.

Also a challenge for solar energy is if it is a cloudy day or it's night time you won't get any energy.

Conclusion and Future of Renewable Energy

In 2024, renewable energy sources provided roughly 22.7% to 24% (energy.gov)

This shows us that we are starting to switch to reneable, but it is going to be hard because our curent infurstructure is already built on nonrenewable energy sources.
